Put your social/volunteering photos in this folder with these exact filenames
so they show up automatically on the Community & Social Impact section:

  winter-relief-1.jpg      -> Winter Relief Distribution photo
  association-1.jpg        -> Gaibandha Students Association event photo
  association-2.jpg        -> Gaibandha Students Association gathering photo
  duke-of-edinburgh.jpg    -> Duke of Edinburgh award activity photo

Any image format works as long as the filename (and .jpg extension) matches.
If you use .png instead, just also update the matching <img src="..."> line
in index.html to end in .png instead of .jpg.
